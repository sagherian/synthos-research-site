Valuation

ARB has the only real income statement in crypto — sequencer gross profit (fee revenue − data-availability cost). It trades at a P/Revenue of 90.7×. The decisive question is value-capture: most L2 tokens are governance-only and do not own the sequencer profit — you are buying an option on the fee switch and on Ethereum's rollup roadmap. Large unlock overhangs make Tokenomics the dominant pillar here. On the Value pillar it screens mid-range versus its sector (46/100 sector-relative).

Network & moat

Honest limits

The token is governance-only today — the protocol's fees do not accrue to you unless a fee switch passes. You are pricing an option, not earnings. Crypto is one liquidity trade in many costumes — in a liquidity drain every sector correlates toward 1, so this rating is relative selection within crypto, not diversification. The macro regime gate sizes total crypto exposure; the score picks within it. Reflexivity breaks models faster than in equities (usage → price → usage).
