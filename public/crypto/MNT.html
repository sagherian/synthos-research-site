The decisive question is value-capture: most L2 tokens are governance-only and do not own the sequencer profit — you are buying an option on the fee switch and on Ethereum's rollup roadmap. Large unlock overhangs make Tokenomics the dominant pillar here. On the Value pillar it screens rich versus its sector (26/100 sector-relative).
