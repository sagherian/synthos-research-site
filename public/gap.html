The Frontier Gap Board

Frontier Gap = scientific feasibility − what the market has priced. Feasibility comes from our frontier-scientist panel (minimum 15 dated claims per field); priced-in is the trade-extension percentile of each field's liquid proxy basket over 10 years. A wide positive Gap = the science is ahead of the trade. Negative = the trade ran ahead of the science.
